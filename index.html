How We Solve It:
40+ Specialized Agents.

Every project runs through our production system — 40+ specialized AI agents organized across five layers, from strategy to final delivery. But agents don't make creative decisions. Our Directors do.

  • Research Agents: Market analysis, competitor audits, trend identification, and audience profiling
  • Script & Brief Generation: AI-drafted creative briefs, storyboards, and script outlines reviewed by human Directors
  • Mood Board Creation: Automated visual reference gathering aligned to brand guidelines
  • Storyboarding: AI-assisted shot planning and narrative sequencing
  • Production Planner: Coordinates all agents, manages timelines, and sequences multi-asset workflows
  • Asset Management: Tracks versions, formats, and delivery specifications across projects
  • Workflow Orchestration: Routes tasks to specialist agents based on project requirements
  • Image Generation: Brand-consistent visuals using multiple AI models, art-directed by specialized agents
  • Video Production: Text-to-video, image-to-video, motion control across multiple platforms
  • Music Production: Full-length original tracks, sound design, and audio beds — no licensing issues
  • Copywriting: Marketing copy, scripts, social captions — human-directed, AI-accelerated
  • Design QA: Automated visual audits against brand guidelines — typography, color, spacing, and composition scoring
  • Content Validation: AI-powered review of generated assets for quality, consistency, and technical compliance
  • Audio Cleaning: Automated level balancing, noise reduction, and format optimization
  • UI/UX Auditing: Interface evaluation against proven UX principles — accessibility, visual hierarchy, and cognitive load
  • Video Compositing: Multi-layer compositions, text overlays, lower thirds, and subtitle burning
  • Motion Graphics: Programmatic video — animated intros, kinetic typography, and branded templates
  • Color Grading: AI-assisted color matching and visual consistency across deliverables
  • Platform Delivery: Auto-formatting for YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, broadcast, and web specs

Everyone has access to AI tools now. The difference isn't the tools — it's who's directing them.

AI can't make you feel something. We can. AI just makes it possible to do more of it.
